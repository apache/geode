Pivotal GemFire Technical Support

Pivotal provides several sources for product information and support. The product's online documentation should always be your first source of information. Pivotal Technical Support engineers will refer you to these documents when applicable. However, you may need to contact Technical Support for the following reasons:

  • Your technical question is not answered in the documentation.
  • You receive an error message that directs you to contact Pivotal Technical Support.
  • You want to report a bug.
  • You want to submit a feature request.

When contacting Pivotal Technical Support, please be prepared to provide the following information:

  • Your name, company name, and Customer ID number,
  • the Pivotal GemFire product and version you are using,
  • the hardware platform, operating system and OS version you are using,
  • a description of the problem or request,
  • exact error message(s) received, if any, and associated logfiles.

Your Pivotal/VMware support agreement may identify specific individuals who are responsible for submitting support requests. If so, please submit your information through those individuals. All responses will be sent to authorized contacts only.

In most cases a Pivotal Support account is required for us to recognize your paid subscription. Note that this is distinct from your Pivotal Network account.

If you have not yet registered, please refer to the articles below.

  • How do I submit a technical support request?
  • Common Support Questions

For all other requests, please contact Pivotal Customer Service at customer-service@pivotal.io.
